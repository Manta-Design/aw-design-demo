About Me

Hi, I'm Austen.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer blending interior architecture, branding, sports graphics, and digital marketing into thoughtful, high-impact visual experiences. MFA-trained with a background in creative strategy, project management, and collaborative design, I'm passionate about creating work that is both visually compelling and purpose-driven.

My path has taken me from studio-based interior design coursework through graduate research to hands-on professional experience — and alongside that, a growing freelance graphic design practice spanning brand identity, athletic branding, and client commissions.
